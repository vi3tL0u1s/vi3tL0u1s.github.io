Viet Hoang Luu

I am a PhD candidate at the University of Melbourne, advised by Dr Amirmohammad Pasdar, Professor Toby Murray, Dr Shaanan Cohney, and Dr Van-Thuan Pham. My research focuses on fuzz testing and software vulnerability detection.

Prior to my PhD, I earned a Master's degree in Cybersecurity from Monash University (2024), graduating as the top student (Dux of Postgraduate). I also hold a Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering from UNSW (2019) and am a GIAC Certified Incident Handler.

I am open to collaboration in:
Fuzz Testing, Static Analysis & Vulnerability Detection
Binary Exploitation & Reverse Engineering

News

  • News [Sep, 2024]: I am glad to say that my first three pull requests were merged into AFLplusplus ( 6.3k): #2540, #2542, #2546
